Instagram Grid Concept

Slow Down at the Shore

A six-post launch grid for Peninsula Del Mar, built to sell a feeling before a room. It alternates scale and intimacy so the story reads as one arc, not six unrelated posts.

01

Arrival: Hero Establishing Shot

Wide drone or golden-hour shot of the full property, coastline, pools, and rooms in one frame. This is the profile's first impression; it needs to feel like scale and stillness, not a brochure.

Caption direction

Short, sensory. Evoke the feeling of arriving, not a list of amenities.

Reference for inspiration

Soneva Resorts and Alila Hotels open their grids with wide, negative-space-heavy shots rather than cramming amenities into frame.

instagram.com/reel/DZUNeOEC3Rs
Caption

peninsuladelmarSome places don't ask for your attention. They just wait for you to arrive. Welcome to the coast, differently.

#PeninsulaDelMar #ConsciousCoast #UdupiCoast

02

The Source: Conscious Coast / Sustainability

Behind-the-scenes of farm-to-table sourcing: a local fisherman, produce being prepped, the chef explaining a dish's origin. This is their stated differentiator that isn't visibly built out yet on their grid.

Caption direction

Story-led, name the person and the source. Human, not a sustainability badge.

Reference for inspiration

Soul & Surf and Suryagarh Jaisalmer treat local sourcing as narrative content, not certification-style messaging.

instagram.com/suryagarh
Caption

peninsuladelmarBefore it reaches your plate, it starts here, with the tide, and the people who know it best. Today's catch, tonight's dinner.

#FarmToTable #ConsciousCoast #FromTheShore

03

Forever, Here: Weddings & Events

A real wedding or styled shoot on the Oceanfront Lawn or in the Elysium Ballroom at golden hour. Events are the highest-revenue lever here, so this post should function as lead-gen, not just pretty content.

Caption direction

Lead with the venue as the star, soft CTA, "DM for venue tours" style, not overtly salesy.

Reference for inspiration

Rambagh Palace and Taj Lake Palace wedding content frames the space as grand but intimate, always selling the venue first.

instagram.com/rambaghpalace
Caption

peninsuladelmarSay it where the tide can hear you. The Oceanfront Lawn is now taking bookings for the 2026 wedding season. DM us for a venue tour.

#PeninsulaWeddings #OceanfrontVows #UdupiWeddings

04

A Day at PDM: POV Guest Experience Reel

A day-in-the-life Reel: sunrise in a Sea View room, breakfast at SOL Cafe, a surf lesson, sunset at Horizon rooftop bar. Sells the entire property in under 20 seconds, not just one room.

Caption direction

Minimal text. Let visuals and a mood-matched trending track carry the pacing.

Reference for inspiration

Ekaa Bali and The Postcard Hotels use fast-cut, guest's-eye POV reels to this exact effect.

instagram.com/reels/DYU8qNesPXF instagram.com/reels/DW3jAlfEYoV
Caption

peninsuladelmarSunrise in your room. Breakfast by the water. A wave before lunch. Sundown at Horizon. One day, fully lived.

#ADayAtPDM #ConsciousCoast #Udupi

05

Salt & Stillness: Wellness / Surf Lifestyle

Yoga at sunrise or a surf lesson, activity-led, not property-led. Broadens the audience beyond couples and wedding guests to solo travelers and wellness seekers.

Caption direction

First-person, aspirational tone. Mornings defined by a wave, not an alarm.

Reference for inspiration

Soul & Surf has built its entire identity around this exact positioning.

instagram.com/reel/DZhm7rvgGIJ instagram.com/soulandsurf instagram.com/p/DYw_rMcMNM1
Caption

peninsuladelmarSome mornings start with an alarm. Here, they start with a wave.

#SaltAndStillness #SurfAndYoga #ConsciousCoast

06

Of This Coast: Community & Local Culture

A real guest's UGC, or a spotlight on something distinctly Udupi: local craft, festival, or cuisine tie-in. Closes the grid on authenticity, signaling the resort is rooted in place, not a generic beach backdrop.

Caption direction

Credit the guest or source, keep tone warm and specific to Udupi.

Reference for inspiration

Kumarakom Lake Resort integrates local culture into its grid in a way that clearly differentiates it from generic beach-resort content.

instagram.com/kumarakomlakeresort
Caption

peninsuladelmarWe didn't choose Udupi. Udupi shaped everything we are. Here's to the coast that raised this place.

#OfThisCoast #Udupi #LocalRoots

Bonus Campaign Ideas

Beyond the launch grid: formats built for reach and a younger, scroll-fast audience. These lean into trends and a more playful, Gen-Z voice, while staying inside the brand's calmer core identity so the account doesn't feel like two different brands.

Trend Remix

"POV: you left your 9 to 5 for this"

Format: Reel, trending audio, on-screen text overlay

A fast-cut Reel using a popular "POV you escaped corporate life" trend format, but shot entirely at the resort: laptop closed, phone on airplane mode, straight into a hammock or the pool. This is the kind of format that gets shared specifically because it's relatable, not because it's beautiful.

Caption

peninsuladelmarquit my job (for the weekend) 🫡 no wifi, no worries, just waves

#QuietQuitting #WeekendReset #PeninsulaDelMar

Interactive

"Rate my room" story series

Format: Instagram Story series with polls/sliders

A recurring Story format where a guest (or staff member) walks through a different room category each week and asks followers to vote: sea view or pool view, this pillow or that one. Low production cost, high interaction, and it doubles as passive research on which room type actually excites the audience most.

Caption

peninsuladelmarhelp us decide bestie, sea view or pool view? 👀 vote before check-in closes

#RateMyRoom #PeninsulaDelMar

Relatable / Meme

"Manifesting this energy" carousel

Format: Carousel, text-forward slide 1

A meme-format carousel: slide one is a bold, aspirational one-liner text card ("me choosing peace over plans"), slides two through four are the actual resort visuals (sunrise, breakfast, rooftop bar). This format performs well because it hooks on the text before the destination even registers, pulling in people who scroll past pure scenery posts.

Caption

peninsuladelmarmanifesting this exact energy for you this week 🌊 (swipe for proof it's real)

#MainCharacterEnergy #ConsciousCoast

UGC Driver

"Tag your travel person" duo challenge

Format: Reel + branded hashtag challenge

A simple, shareable prompt: guests film a two-second reaction the moment they see their room or the oceanfront lawn, then tag the friend or partner they wish was there. It's built to be re-shared into DMs, which is the real engine of reach for a resort that doesn't yet have a large following of its own.

Caption

peninsuladelmartag the person you're dragging here next 📍 we'll wait

#TagYourTravelPerson #PeninsulaDelMar

Countdown / Urgency

"Wedding season is booking up" sticker series

Format: Story countdown sticker + Reel recap

A limited-run Story series counting down open wedding dates on the Oceanfront Lawn for the upcoming season, paired with a short recap Reel of past setups. Creates real urgency for planners without needing paid ads, and it's a format planners already expect from venues they follow.

Caption

peninsuladelmar3 dates left before the lawn is fully booked for wedding season 👰🤵 swipe up to check availability

#PeninsulaWeddings #BookingUpFast

Grid rhythm: hero, human story, revenue driver, motion, lifestyle, community. Built to reach couples, wedding planners, wellness travelers, and locals within one scroll.
